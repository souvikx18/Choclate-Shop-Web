Chocolate History

When did chocolates first appear in India?

Chocolates were introduced to India during the British colonial period. Initially, they were a luxury item enjoyed only by the elite and British officials. The first significant entry into the Indian market came when Cadbury started selling chocolates in India around 1948. Over time, chocolates became affordable and popular, becoming one of the most loved sweets in India. (Source: Wikipedia)
